INTEGRATED PROJECT MANAGEMENT TRAINING CURRICULUM

Introduction to Project Management

Learn the basics of Project Management,hoe the time,scope,budget and stakeholders are managed

Stakeholder Management

Stakeholder Management accounts for the success or failure of Projects.Understanding stackholder mapping will go a long way in increasing probability of success

Agile Project Management

There has been an increasing need for faster execution and solution delivery.Getting things done faster with quick feedbacks from customer is possible with Agile PM

Project Reporting

Reporting Projects status is an effective way of keeping key project stakeholders informed.Knowing when and how to report to whom is super critical.

MS Project Training

Learn how to plan massive /complex projects using MS Project.Critical path method and network diagrams helps to see what's important.Tasks,dependencies,resources,gantt charts,etc.

Project Management Framework (PRINCE2 and PMP)

Have a deep dive into the two leading project management certifications.See how their exhaustive and very useful frameworks has been applied in different project scenariois.
